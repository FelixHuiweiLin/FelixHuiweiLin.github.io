Biography

I am come from Xingtai, Hebei. I am currently a Third-year PhD student in School of School of Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, under the supervision by Prof. Yunming Ye. My current research interests span computer vision and machine learning with a series of topics, such as image recognition, remote sensing scene recognition, meta learning, few-shot learning, zero-shot learning.
