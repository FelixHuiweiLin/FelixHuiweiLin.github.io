Biography

Felix Huiwei Lin is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Sinno Jialin Pan. Before that, he obtained his Ph.D. degree from the Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) under the supervision of Prof. Yunming Ye and Prof. Shanshan Feng. His current research interests include informed machine learning and continual learning.
